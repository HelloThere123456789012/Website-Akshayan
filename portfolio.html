About Me

A Little More About Me

Hi! I’m someone who’s always been curious. Whether it’s figuring out how a website works, how to draw a new character, or how to play a song by ear—I’m constantly exploring new skills and ideas.

Creativity is My Foundation
Ever since I was a kid, I’ve loved drawing and making things. Art taught me how to see details, how to be patient, and how to express what I feel. Over time, that love of creation spread into everything I do.

Problem Solving Through Programming
I got into programming because I wanted to build things. I enjoy thinking logically and turning ideas into something that works on screen. Coding gives me the tools to bring imagination to life—and that’s powerful.

Soundtrack of My Life
Music helps me focus, relax, and sometimes even code better. I play both keyboard and guitar, and I’m always messing around with melodies or learning songs I love. Music is one of my favorite ways to feel connected to everything.

Movement Matters
Playing badminton gives me a mental break from screens and helps me stay balanced. I like the mix of speed, strategy, and rhythm in the game—it’s both physical and thoughtful, like a dance you control with precision.

Why This Site Exists
This site is where I bring it all together. It’s a place where I can showcase what I do, track what I’m learning, and share it with the world. Whether you’re here to explore, collaborate, or just say hi—I’m glad you made it.
